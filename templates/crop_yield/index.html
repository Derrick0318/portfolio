Quick Start Guide

1

Upload Your Image

Click Upload & Analyze in the sidebar. Drag and drop — or click the upload area — to select satellite or drone imagery. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, TIF, TIFF, WebP. You can upload multiple images at once.

2

Run AI Analysis

Click PROCESS ANALYSIS. The AI model extracts vegetation indices (NDVI, EVI) and colour features from the image to estimate crop health and yield potential.

3

Add GPS Coordinates (Optional)

For richer predictions, click Add Location on any result card and enter your field's latitude and longitude. This fetches live weather data and soil type for your area.

4

Review Results & Recommendations

Each result card shows the Predicted Yield (T/Ha), a Yield Class (Good / Fair / Poor), top recommended crops, and an environment snapshot with weather and soil data.

5

View & Export History

All predictions are saved in History. Click any history item to expand the full result. Use Export to download a CSV of all predictions.

About This System

AI Model

Random Forest Regressor + Classifier trained on Malaysian multi-crop agricultural datasets with image feature extraction pipeline.

Weather Data

7-day forecast sourced from the Open-Meteo API using your GPS coordinates for temperature, precipitation and humidity.

Soil Data

Soil texture and pH data retrieved from the SoilGrids / ISRIC World Soil Database using coordinate-based lookup.

Version

Crop Yield AI v2.0 — Final Year Project (FYP). Built with Python Flask, scikit-learn, and Vanilla JS.
